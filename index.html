We only own 7% of the businesses in America

We are 13% of the American population. Therefore, we have room for improvement in terms of strengthening ourselves economically. Nsite wants you to do at least one of two things. Donate to black-owned businesses everywhere as well as the ones showcased by Nsite and/or start your own business which Nsite would be more than willing to support.

Did you know?

MORE THAN 90% OF BLACK-OWNED BUSINESSES DO NOT HAVE PAID EMPLOYEES. Most black-owned businesses are lead by those who are self-employed, due to a lack of capital and/or revenue. This causes the owner of that business to be responsible for all functions. Nsite wants you to help fund these businesses so that they may accumulate capital and also consume from these businesses to increase their revenue. An increased bottom-line will allow our businesses to hire employees which will not only decrease the stress of our business owners, but it will also go a long way in fighting against black unemployment.

About Nsite

My name is Jason Scott and NSITE is a support group that provides funding for black businesses. The general idea is for us to find black businesses seeking funding online via sites such as gofundme. We also want to encourage new businesses that aren't currently using the internet to get funding to do so. When we discover a black business that is moving in the right direction but just needs the funding to get started, our group will donate to that business.

NSITE comes from the words insight and incite. To have insight is to have understanding of the foundation of something. Incite means to influence violence or some form of outrage. African people must have the insight to understand the foundation of why we think the way that we do and then incite mental warfare on the old way of thinking.
